Hello!

I'm a creative game programmer who wants to make immersive games that people love to play. I specialize in traditional 2D and 3D games using C# and Unity, but have also built a virtual reality game in Unreal Engine 4 using visual scripting blueprints.

I'm organized and love solving problems and puzzles of all sorts. Thanks to my background in customer service, I have strong communication skills and 8 years of experience working as part of a team in challenging environments.

What I can do.

I have successfully worked on many different aspects of video games, including design, game management, textures, UI/UX, and sound.

I believe that the most important part of any game is the user experience, so I focus my efforts on developing the game's look and feel.

I also dabble in website design/building and SQL. I'm currently learning C++ in my spare time.
