Mr. Skellington

You play as Mr. Skellington, a skeleton who wants nothing more than health in his old age. Luckily, milk can help create strong bones and he happened to find a cave full of it! Your goal is to collect all of the milk cartons in the cave, hopping from platform to platform and timing your jump onto the elevator to reach cartons on higher levels.

Process

This game began a sprite animation script and a desire to create a simple 2D platformer with collectables. I like to make things look good, so my first step was taking care of all the visual content. I found basic cave textures and a free-to-use sprite sheet for Mr. Skellington, then used Photoshop to make them seem more cohesive. I also made the collectables and all of the UI screens using Photoshop.
